Amirhossein Noohian

My research interests lie in the areas of machine learning, controls, and robotics. I am interested in using both machine learning and control methods to develop the algorithms that enhance the efficieny, safety, and autonomy of robots.

In 2022, I recieved an M.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering from Sharif University of Technology. During my M.Sc. studies, I conducted research on reinforcement learning and learning from demonstration in robotics under the supervision of Professor Saeed Khodaygan. I previously received a B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering from Amirkabir University of Technology in 2019.

At present, I work remotely as a Graduate Research Assistant at University of Surreyunder the supervision of Professor Saber Fallah. We are developing safe and efficient robot learning algorithms using model predictive control and reinforcement learning.
